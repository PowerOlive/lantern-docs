Google Authentication Refresh Token x x - If you are a user with your own dedicated fallback proxy, your fallback proxy stores and forwards kaleidoscope notifications on your behalf while your own Lantern client is offline.  This ensures that people don’t miss notifications about new users in the trust network (which come over Google chat).  In order to do this, the fallback proxies hold on to a refresh token that gives them access to your Google Talk account.
